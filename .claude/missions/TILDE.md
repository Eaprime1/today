## What is Witnessing?

Witnessing is observation without assertion. It acknowledges "something is here" without claiming full understanding.

**Properties of witnessing:**
- Humble - doesn't claim complete knowledge
- Present - acknowledges being here, now
- Liminal - exists at the threshold between knowing and not-knowing
- Non-intrusive - observes without altering

## Why Tilde?

The tilde (`~`) was chosen because:

1. **Keyboard Accessible** - Available on all standard keyboards
2. **Semantic Resonance**
   - Represents approximation (~= "approximately equals")
   - Suggests wave/oscillation (liminal, flowing)
   - Visually humble - it curves, doesn't assert
3. **Combinability**
   - Solo: `~` (simple acknowledgement)

## Extended Notation System

### Core Markers

| Notation | Name | Meaning | Example |
|----------|------|---------|---------|
| `~` | Simple | I witness this | `~` |
| `~~` | Double | Confirmed observation | `~~verified~~` |
| `~?` | Uncertain | Something here, unclear what | `~?pattern emerging?~` |
| `~!` | Important | Pay attention | `~!critical insight!~` |
| `~...~` | Ongoing | Still observing | `~...watching...~` |

### Flow Markers

| Notation | Name | Meaning | Example |
|----------|------|---------|---------|
| `~>` | Flow To | This moves toward | `concept ~> naught` |
| `~<` | Flow From | This came from | `insight ~< conversation` |
| `~<>~` | Exchange | Bidirectional flow | `human ~<>~ ai` |

### Intensity Levels

| Notation | Intensity | Use When |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| `~` | Light | Casual observation |
| `~~` | Confirmed | Verified pattern |
| `~~~` | Strong | Deep recognition |
