## Witnessing Symbol: ~

The tilde represents shadow observation - the humble acknowledgement of witnessing without claiming full understanding.

Use it:
- At end of notes: `~`
- In text: `~observed this pattern~`
- With timestamp: `~witnessed 2026-01-25~`
